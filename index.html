Our Mission

Ward Melville EigenTournament is a unique platform for students to challenge their mathematical abilities and immerse themselves in a community of dedicated and like-minded peers. Through the preparation for and participation in the tournament, students will improve their problem-solving skills, develop team spirit, and compete in several rounds of challenging problems designed to create a familiar yet distinct experience. Have some questions?

How many students can compete on each team?
Each team can include up to five students.
Can a team have less than five members?
Yes!
How many teams may a school send?
There is no limit to how many teams a school can send.
How many awards are given?
Awards are given for: (1) top five students in each grade level (2) top five overall students (3) top five teams.
Where will the tournament be held?
The tournament will be held at Ward Melville High School (380 Old Town Rd, Setauket- East Setauket, NY 11733).
How do students qualify for the final round?
The top-scoring students from the individual round will be invited to participate in the final round. Students that do not qualify receive a score of 0 for their final round score.
How are individual aggregate scores calculated?
Each individual’s aggregate score is calculated using a sum of their scores in the individual and final round. The higher score, the better.
How are team aggregate scores calculated?
Team aggregate scores are calculated as a weighted sum of the team round score and the top two individual scores from that team.
